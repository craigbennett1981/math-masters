Privacy Policy for Math Masters

Last Updated: August 29, 2025

Welcome to Math Masters ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our application.

1. Data We Collect

Our goal is to provide a fun and educational experience while respecting your privacy. We collect a limited amount of non-personal, anonymous data to improve our app's performance and stability.

a. Analytics Data (via Vexo)

We use Vexo Analytics to understand how our app is used. This service helps us see which features are popular, how much time users spend in the app, and general usage patterns. All data collected by Vexo is anonymous and aggregated, meaning it cannot be used to identify you personally.

b. Diagnostics Data (via Sentry.io)

We use Sentry.io to monitor the app for crashes and errors. If the app crashes, Sentry collects anonymous diagnostic information, such as device type, operating system version, and technical error logs. This data is essential for helping us find and fix bugs to improve the app's stability. It does not contain any personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

The anonymous data we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To improve app features, performance, and user experience.
  • To identify and resolve technical issues, bugs, and crashes.
  • To make informed decisions about future updates and developments.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Vexo Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics. You can view their policy at vexo.co/privacy.
  • Sentry.io: For anonymous crash reporting and diagnostics. You can view their policy at sentry.io/privacy/.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. All data collected is anonymous and used solely for improving the app.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.
