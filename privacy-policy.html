Privacy Policy for Math Masters

Effective Date: August 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Math Masters ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in our application Math Masters: Fun with Numbers (the "App"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about the data we collect.

1. Data We Collect

To help us improve the Math Masters app and provide a better experience, we collect anonymous analytics data. We do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email address, location, or other sensitive personal data.

The anonymous data we collect includes:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, such as features used, screens viewed, session duration, and actions taken within the game.
  • Device Information: General information about your device, including the device model, operating system version, and screen resolution.
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and other performance data to help us identify and fix bugs.

All game progress and scores are still stored locally on your device only and are not transmitted to us.

2. How We Use Your Data

The anonymous data we collect is used solely for internal purposes to:

  • Understand how our users engage with the App.
  • Improve the App's features, design, and overall user experience.
  • Diagnose and resolve technical issues and crashes.

3. Third-Party Services (Analytics)

We use Vexo as our third-party analytics provider to collect and process this anonymous data. Vexo has its own privacy policy that governs its use of data.

You can review Vexo's privacy policy here: https://vexo.co/privacy

4. Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. The anonymous analytics data we collect is not used to identify or track individual children. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages and is in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of the app on the App Store. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: support@mathmasters.app
